## What's New

- **Vehicle access logging**: Track vehicle entries and exits with licence plate recognition
- **Person access tracking**: Log person access events across your sites
- **Access analytics**: View entry/exit patterns, peak times, and activity trends
- **Vehicle image preview**: View captured vehicle images in expanded modal
- **Site and staff activity**: See scan activity breakdown by site and staff member
- **Flagged vehicle alerts**: Identify flagged vehicles for enhanced security
- **Export functionality**: Export access logs with toast notifications for status

## Improvements

- **Better filtering**: Enhanced filtering system with proper data fetching
- **Improved error handling**: Better error recovery and loading states
- **Standardized display**: Consistent formatting when information is not available
- **Navigation improvements**: Better modal navigation for reviewing access events
