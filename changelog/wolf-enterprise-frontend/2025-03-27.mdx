- **Areas on live map**: Geographic areas now display on the live map with labels

- **Area navigation**: Click areas to navigate and zoom to their location
- **Report scrolling**: Reports automatically scroll into view when selected

- **Sidebar scrolling**: Fixed infinite scroll issues in sidebar
- **Icons in headings**: Added visual icons to section headings
- **Point styling**: Grey out inactive points for better clarity
