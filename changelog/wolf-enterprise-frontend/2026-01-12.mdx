## What's New

- **Outcomes viewer**: Table of submissions with dates, ratings, concerns, environmental factors, and locations.
- **Analytics dashboard**: KPIs, rating distributions, and breakdowns of concerns and environment.
- **Interactive map**: Heatmap and pin modes for submission locations.

## Improvements

- **Unified filters**: One filter panel for outcomes, analytics, and map.
- **Advanced filters**: By link, submission type, date range, day/night ratings, concerns, environment, and more.
- **Search**: Across notes, concerns, and environmental fields.
- **Details**: Drill into a submission for full notes and location.
- **Pagination**: Cursor-based for large datasets.

## User experience

- Tabbed layout for links, outcomes, analytics, and map.
- Colour-coded ratings for quick scanning.
- Collapsible advanced filters to keep the UI tidy.
