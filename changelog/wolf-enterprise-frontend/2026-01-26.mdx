## What's New

### Email connectors

- **Connector builder**: Create connectors with a guided UI and template presets.
- **Test email**: Verify setups before activation.
- **Placeholders**: Organisation-specific placeholders in templates.

### Report intelligence

- **Dashboard**: Interactive graphs for report networks and relationships.
- **Map view**: Locations and patterns with filtering and focus.
- **Alert metrics**: Delivery counts and performance alongside intelligence views.
- **Patrol comments**: Add and view comments on patrol events; badges show threads with notes.
