## What's New

### Field survey (mobile)

- **Safety survey tool**: Geolocated community safety perception data while moving through neighbourhoods.
- **Day and night ratings**: Emoji-based scales for how safe an area feels by time of day.
- **Languages**: English, Afrikaans, isiZulu, and isiXhosa.
- **Observed concerns**: Quick tags for issues such as loitering, lighting, or abandoned buildings.
- **Location capture**: GPS with each submission for mapping and analysis.

### Public links (admin)

- **Survey settings**: Create and manage public survey links from a dedicated admin area.
- **Named links**: Multiple links per organisation for campaigns and tracking.
- **One-click sharing**: Copy full URLs or short links from the dashboard.
- **Public survey page**: Mobile-optimised survey for anyone with the link—no login.
- **Staff survey shortcut**: Open the authenticated staff survey from settings when needed.

## Improvements

- **Field UX**: Weather-style day/night cards, rapid collection with short cooldown, offline-friendly language preference, compact single-screen layout.
- **Public messaging**: Clear mission copy; GPS status only when relevant; multilingual public surveys; Business Tools entry for settings.

## When to use

- **Staff survey (login)**: Structured audits, known submitters, training, higher accountability.
- **Public links (no login)**: Community campaigns, social and print, QR codes, maximum reach.
