## What's New

### Radar analytics

- **Radar tab**: Analytics for monitored WhatsApp groups—keywords, sentiment, and activity patterns.
- **Keyword tracking**: Watchlist phrases with frequency and change-over-time views.
- **Sentiment breakdown**: Automatic positive / neutral / negative split across messages.
- **Participant activity**: See who is most active and how it relates to tracked keywords.
- **Trending terms**: Frequently used terms across monitored groups.

### Message feeds

- **Message feeds**: Multiple groups side by side in a multi-column layout.
- **Feed decks**: Save and switch named feed configurations for different monitoring setups.
- **Flexible columns**: Add groups as columns (including multi-select) to build views quickly.

## Improvements

- **Radar**: 7–14–30–60 day windows; scope by feed decks or individual groups; substring vs word match; trend deltas vs prior period.
- **Feeds**: Live refresh as messages arrive; layout polish for wide boards on smaller screens.
