## What's New

- **Patrol route creation**: Create and manage patrol routes with interactive maps
- **Checkpoint management**: Add checkpoints with custom settings including radius, terrain type, and instructions
- **Patrol schedules**: Schedule patrols with flexible timing options
- **Route visualization**: See patrol routes displayed on interactive maps with distance calculations
- **Patrol assignments**: Assign staff to patrol routes and track their progress
- **Event tracking**: Log patrol events including checkpoint arrivals and departures

## Improvements

- **Enhanced access control**: Better permission handling for patrol management features
- **Improved map functionality**: Better checkpoint rendering and route display
