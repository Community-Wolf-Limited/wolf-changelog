## Improvements

- **Message ordering**: Conversation history now displays messages in correct chronological order, making it easier to follow the conversation flow.
- **Human message indicators**: Messages sent by human operators are now clearly marked with a visual indicator, making it easy to distinguish between automated and human responses.

## Bug Fixes

- **Message sequencing**: Fixed issue where messages appeared out of order in the conversation history, ensuring users can follow emergency conversations accurately.
