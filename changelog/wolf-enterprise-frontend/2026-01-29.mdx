## What's New

- **Emergency workflows**: Messaging and agent controls support urgent scenarios with clearer handoff.
- **Connector email**: Outbound connector mail uses plain text for broader mail-client compatibility.

## Improvements

- **Conversation order**: History lists messages in stable chronological order.
- **Human messages**: Visual distinction for messages sent by people versus automation.
- **Thread sync**: History stays aligned so teams see one coherent timeline.
- Release integration: `coverage-fixes-dedup`.

## Bug Fixes

- **Sequencing**: Fewer out-of-order or duplicated entries in fast-moving threads.
- **History**: Activity flags and history stay in sync across views.
