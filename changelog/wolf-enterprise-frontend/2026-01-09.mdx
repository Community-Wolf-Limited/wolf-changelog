## What's New

### Organisation branding

- **Custom styling**: Colours, logo, font, and corner roundness for public surveys.
- **Organisation profile**: Dedicated area for organisation details and public branding.
- **Fonts**: Broad font list for consistent on-brand surveys.
- **Corner radius**: Presets from none through full rounding across survey UI.

### Access control images

- **Secure loading**: Time-limited URLs and clearer loading states in galleries.
- **Consistent previews**: Licence disc and person images share the same secure loading behaviour.

## Improvements

- **Navigation**: Clearer organisation profile entry points and tab labels.
- **Light defaults**: Public surveys default to a clean light theme matching the platform.
- **Reset**: Reset to defaults preserves whether custom branding is enabled.

## How to configure branding

1. Open **Settings → Organisation profile**
2. Open the **Public branding** tab
3. Turn **Custom branding** on
4. Adjust colours, logo, font, and corner radius
5. **Save branding** to apply to public surveys
