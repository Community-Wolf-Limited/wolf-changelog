## What's new

- **Guided setup**: A short, ordered wizard walks partners from creating a key through their first successful call and map integration, instead of a long flat docs page.
- **Try it live**: An in-browser playground lets partners build requests, see responses, and pick hexes on a map— including handing off a newly created key automatically.
- **Keys at a glance**: Filter keys by lifecycle state (active, rotating, inactive) so large teams can see what needs attention.
- **Who can do what**: Members and admins see the right navigation; sensitive areas stay behind admin access.
- **Org clarity**: Organisation names resolve consistently in the switcher, and platform-admin flows handle reassignment without confusing conflicts.

## Improvements

- **Observability in the product**: Logs, traces, and summarized operational views live in the portal. Earlier experiments with embedding a full Grafana-style stack were **removed** in favour of these first-party views and metrics.
- **Marketing home**: A richer landing experience for the portal with motion and visual polish.
- **Stability**: Fewer hydration and auth edge cases in settings and the playground; CI and tests tightened.

## Bug fixes

- Fixed cases where organisation labels appeared as opaque ids instead of readable names.
- Addressed playground authentication and key-format hints so failed calls are easier to diagnose.
- Settings deep links and form semantics improved for accessibility and routing.
