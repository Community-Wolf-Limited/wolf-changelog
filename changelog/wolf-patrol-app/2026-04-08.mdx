## What's new

- **Account access**: Email and password sign-in with a phone path if the primary flow is slow or unavailable.
- **Operations overview**: Home shows how sites are performing against expectations, key numbers at a glance, and recent patrol activity—with filters you can keep between sessions.
- **Schedules end-to-end**: Create, review, edit, and remove schedules from the app with native pickers and a guided wizard shared across flows.
- **Clearer forms**: Searchable pickers for places, routes, people, and time zones; required fields are obvious; drafts survive switching apps or taking a call.
- **Routes**: Rename and renumber checkpoints more predictably; open a new route right after saving; trigger a patrol from the detail screen when needed.
- **Multi-org**: Switch organisation from the More area when your account spans several tenants.

## Improvements

- List screens show helpful empty states instead of blank pages.
- Patrol lists load without blocking the map on very large organisations.

## Bug fixes

- Android devices (including Honor / MagicUI) behave better when the keyboard opens—fields stay visible and scrolling feels natural.
- Splash and loading states no longer stick on a white screen while authentication initialises.
- Build and runtime issues resolved for current Expo, Hermes, and Clerk combinations.
- Default access is **unlocked** for patrol configuration unless your organisation intentionally enforces stricter rules offline.

## Removed

- The old **field grant / permission gate** (extra tabs, overlays, and background checks) is gone—patrol staff can work without that layer.
- **Always-on idle GPS tracking** is removed to protect battery; location use is tied to meaningful patrol workflows instead of continuous background polling.
