## What's new

- **Scoped capabilities**: Partner keys can enable or disable feature families; missing scope returns a clear denial instead of ambiguous errors.
- **Organisation context**: Platform operators see directory information, can adjust org metadata, and get per-org counters and log streams suited to support workflows.
- **Tracing**: Request spans can be exported to a standard trace backend for operators who wire it up.
- **Safer operations**: Containers run without root; deployments follow a staged, memory-aware recipe; a small CLI helps smoke-test health and scoring after releases.

## Improvements

- **Observability without the LGTM experiment**: Metrics, logs, and summarized trace data remain first-class; the bundled **Grafana** service and dashboards from the short-lived LGTM-style experiment were **removed**. Prometheus-compatible metrics and log/trace pipelines remain where operators need them.
- Batch and payload limits on scoring endpoints reduce accidental overload.
- Corridor-style routing fills in tier detail even for degenerate origin/destination cases.

## Bug fixes

- Browser clients on localhost are less likely to hit CORS pitfalls during early onboarding.
- Mongo connection lifecycle closes cleanly on shutdown.
- Origin validation is stricter; TLS/site configuration docs and scripts help avoid wrong certificates on shared hosts.

## Removed

- **Grafana** as a packaged dependency of the default compose story (part of the abandoned LGTM product experiment); other telemetry components may still be used by operators directly.
