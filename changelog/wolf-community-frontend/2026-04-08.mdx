## What's new

- **Export from reports**: Download report data in common formats when you are signed in, with clear errors if something blocks the download.
- **Easier scanning**: Columns, filters, and date headers are structured so long reports are easier to read and sort.

## Improvements

- Hex resolution now follows the visible map area more intelligently, aligning with the enterprise map and avoiding artificial caps that left holes on wide viewports.
- National overlays are built after the full dataset is ready, so tiles do not appear “patchy” while loading.
- Partner integrations that need scoped keys for map scoring are supported without exposing internal wiring.

## Bug fixes

- Fixed visible gaps in crime hex coverage on large monitors at high zoom.
