## What's New

- **Instance-specific calendar filtering**: Filter your calendar view to show specific schedule instances
- **Empty calendar option**: Start with a blank calendar when creating new schedules
- **Improved schedule descriptions**: Better visibility of schedule details and descriptions

## Improvements

- **Better organization switching**: Smoother experience when switching between organizations as an admin
- **Enhanced phone validation**: Improved validation for phone number fields
- **Schedule instance editing**: Fixed various issues when editing individual schedule instances
