## What's New

- **Interactive route creation**: Build patrol routes visually on an interactive map
- **Checkpoint management**: Add, edit, and reorder checkpoints with drag-and-drop support
- **Route statistics**: View distance, estimated time, and checkpoint count for each route
- **Terrain-based calculations**: Walking speeds automatically adjusted based on terrain type
- **Route validation**: Automatic validation ensures routes meet requirements before saving
- **Collapsible panels**: Better screen space usage with collapsible route management panels
