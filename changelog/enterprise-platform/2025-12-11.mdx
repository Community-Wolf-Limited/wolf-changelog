## What's New

- **Live patrol event updates**: Patrol events now appear instantly on your screen as they happen - no more manual refreshing needed
- **Real-time access control logs**: Vehicle and person access logs update automatically as scans occur at your sites

## Improvements

- **Faster data loading**: Access control screens now load more efficiently with improved data handling
- **Better duplicate prevention**: System now intelligently filters duplicate events to keep your feeds clean
